Deep-End Weavers

Our Story:
Welcome to the Deep-End Weavers, where we believe that the best baskets are made underwater, and a sense of humor is as essential as oxygen tanks! Founded by a group of maverick mermaids and a rogue dolphin with a flair for crafts, we've been teaching the ancient art of underwater basket weaving since we discovered that WiFi works under the sea (who knew?). Our classes are held in the most serene underwater locations, where the only distraction might be a curious fish or two. Our team comprises expert weavers, seasoned scuba divers, and at least one person who's here just because they took a wrong turn at the coral reef. Together, we bring you the most unconventional crafting experience on (or under) the planet. Why underwater basket weaving, you ask? Well, if you can weave a basket under water, just imagine how good you'll be on dry land! Plus, it's the only course where you can say, “I’m literally in over my head,” and mean it both figuratively and literally. Join us for a splashy adventure into the depths of creativity. Whether you're here to find your inner peace among the seaweed, or you've always wondered if baskets hold water (spoiler: they don’t), the Deep-End Weavers promise you an experience that's as unique as a starfish's fingerprint. Dive in – the water's fine, and the baskets are just waiting to be woven!
